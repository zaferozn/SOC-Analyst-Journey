# Day 3 - English Drill

## Objective
Practice SOC analyst writing for failed and successful SSH login patterns.

## Analyst Writing Patterns
- The logs showed...
- This pattern may indicate...
- The successful login should be reviewed to determine whether...

## Practice Summary
The logs showed 23 failed SSH login attempts and 1 successful SSH login attempt within the last 20 minutes. This pattern may indicate possible account compromise. The successful login should be reviewed to determine whether it was authorized.

## Key Takeaway
Several failed SSH login attempts followed by a successful SSH login may indicate possible account compromise. The successful login should be reviewed to determine whether it was authorized.
