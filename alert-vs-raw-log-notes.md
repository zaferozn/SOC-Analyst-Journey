# Alert vs Raw Log Notes

## Raw Log
Raw logs contain detailed original event data. They may include a large amount of information that is difficult to interpret at the beginning.

## Alert
An alert is a processed or highlighted signal based on certain log patterns or rule logic. It helps the analyst focus on activity that may require attention.
