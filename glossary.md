# SOC Glossary

- **Alert**: A notification triggered by suspicious or notable activity.
- **Authentication**: The process of verifying identity.
- **Failed login**: An unsuccessful authentication attempt.
- **Source IP**: The IP address from which the activity originated.
- **Suspicious**: Activity that appears abnormal or potentially malicious.
- **Investigation**: The process of examining evidence to understand an event.
- **Brute force**: Repeated attempts to guess credentials.
- **Evidence**: Data or artifacts used to support analysis.
